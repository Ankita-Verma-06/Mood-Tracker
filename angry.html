Signs of Anger

Recognizing the signs of anger can help you manage it better:

  • Emotional Signs: Irritability, frustration, anxiety, or feeling overwhelmed.
  • Physical Signs: Increased heart rate, clenched fists, sweating, or tension in your body.

Techniques to Manage Anger

Here are some ways to manage anger:

  • Take deep breaths and count to 10 before reacting.
  • Go for a walk or engage in physical activity to release tension.
  • Write down your feelings in a journal to process them.
  • Practice mindfulness or meditation to calm your mind.
  • Use positive self-talk to reframe your thoughts.
  • Seek support from a friend or therapist.
